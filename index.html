Holland's Floral Design

The perfect place for all your floral needs.

Welcome to Holland’s Floral Design, where elegant floral designs and sufficient prices meet. Located on the central coast, where the weather is perfect for numerous florals and plants to thrive. Holland’s Floral Design is a small independent business that is run by close knit friends and family members. At Holland’s Floral Design, each plant and floral is given the love and care it deserves from the very beginning of its life. Every plant is organically grown and specifically selected based off it’s seasonal growth patterns. Not only are each plants grown with care, but they are also hand picked and pruned with the most tender instructions. This love and care is shown through the stunning design that your events of any kind needs! Book your floral needs for any type of event; weddings, bridal shower, funeral, birthday party, baby shower, and so more. We want our flower shop to be portrayed with a small hometown feel that provides excellent services for events and for customers who just genuinely love plants and flowers! Our services are for those who want quality products but do not necessarily need a deep pocket to get it. Holland’s Floral Design will work with any budget, this is because we know the importance to working with the diversity of our customers. Also, the variety of products available can suffice any budget or event.
